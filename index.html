About The Conference

Al-Azhar is one of the oldest famous mosques and universities all over the world. It was established more than one thousand years ago (970-972 AD), therefore, it is the oldest in Cairo. Studying sections were assigned inside Al-Azhar mosque since it was established. Recently, a new sector has been introduced into Al Azhar University implying Engineering since 1964. Al Azhar Engineering International Conference (AEIC) started since 1989 to be held regularly. Uniquely, the conference

F.A.Q

  • What are the Conference topics ?
    • Computer Systems Engineering
    • Civil engineering
    • Electrical engineering
    • Mechanical engineering
    • Mining, Petroleum and Metallurgical Engineering
    • Architectural engineering
    • Urban Planning Engineering
  • What are the Conference key dates?
    • Call for abstract: March 2020
    • Deadline for abstract submission: 1/6/2020
    • Abstract acceptance notification: 1/7/2020
    • Deadline for final paper submission: 1/9/2020
    • Announcement of full paper acceptance: 1/10/2020
    • Conference dates: 12-14 December 2020
  • What are the Conference Objectives?
    • To learn about the latest research developments in the field of applied engineering sciences.
    • To learn about scientific researches that integrate different engineering disciplines and highlight their impacts on engineering education.
    • To highlight the impact of recent industry trends on integrated engineering education curricula.
    • To highlight common concepts among different engineering fields to support advanced technological industries.
    • To identify currently applied fields in engineering sciences to update the academic engineering curricula.
  • What are the Conference activities?
    • Plenary sessions to present keynote speakers talk.
    • Technical sessions to discuss the accepted papers for oral presentation.
    • A series of seminars on topics of importance to achieve Egypt's Vision 2030 within the framework of engineering education
  • Participants

    Participants include researchers, academics and experts, from inside and outside Egypt, who are interested in the engineering education and integration issues; at the local, regional and international levels. Participants also include representatives of international and regional bodies and organizations interested in engineering education and sustainable development issues, in addition to representatives of the concerned ministries and national bodies, and representatives from the public and private sectors.

  • Languages

    All manuscripts must be clearly written in Arabic or in English. The conference provides interpretation services.
