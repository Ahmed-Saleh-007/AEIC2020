About The Conference

Under the auspices of Prof. Dr. Ahmed Al-Tayeb, the Grand Imam of Al-Azhar al-Sharif and under the aegis of Prof. Dr. Mohammed Al-Mahrasawi, the President of Al-Azhar University. The Faculty of Engineering will hold its fifteenth bi-annual conference. Since the scientific research has the utmost importance in the fields of engineering, and industry; the professors of the Faculty of Engineering have taken a vow since the first conference took place in 1989 to hold Al-Azhar International Engineering Conference (AEIC) every two years. And this is the fifteenth conference will conduct on March 13, 2021. entitled "The Holistic Engineering" Towards the Achievement of Egypt 2030 Vision. AEIC is a major forum for the presentation of innovative ideas, approaches, developments, and research projects in multiple fields of engineering and complementary studies like engineering mathematics. AEIC serves to facilitate the exchange of information between researchers and industry professionals to discuss the latest issues and advancement in the field of engineering innovations. In addition, the conference will have multiple plenary sessions to bring together researchers and scientists from different fields. Therefore, researchers are invited to contribute to the conference by participating in original research that contributes to developing the fields of applied engineering.

    • Architectural engineering
    • Urban Planning Engineering
  • What are the Conference key dates?
    • Call for abstract: 15 Aug. 2020
    • Deadline for abstract submission: 11 Dec. 2020
    • Abstract acceptance notification: 17 Dec. 2020
    • Deadline for final paper submission: 23 Jan. 2021
    • Announcement of full paper acceptance: 21 Feb. 2021
    • Conference dates: 13-15 Mar. 2021
  • What are the Conference Objectives?
    • To learn about the latest research developments in the field of applied engineering sciences.
    • To learn about scientific researches that integrate different engineering disciplines and highlight their impacts on engineering education.
    • To highlight the impact of recent industry trends on integrated engineering education curricula.
    • To highlight common concepts among different engineering fields to support advanced technological industries.
    • To identify currently applied fields in engineering sciences to update the academic engineering curricula.
  • What are the Conference activities?
    • Plenary sessions to present keynote speakers talk.
    • Technical sessions to discuss the accepted papers for oral presentation.
    • A series of seminars on topics of importance to achieve Egypt's Vision 2030 within the framework of engineering education
  • Participants

    Participants include researchers, academics and experts, from inside and outside Egypt, who are interested in the engineering education and integration issues; at the local, regional and international levels. Participants also include representatives of international and regional bodies and organizations interested in engineering education and sustainable development issues, in addition to representatives of the concerned ministries and national bodies, and representatives from the public and private sectors.

  • Languages

    All manuscripts must be clearly written in Arabic or in English. The conference provides interpretation services.
